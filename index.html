About Our Club

Who We Are

RJIT Geeks Club is a vibrant community of passionate coders and tech enthusiasts at RJIT. We aim to foster a culture of innovation, learning, and collaboration among students interested in programming and technology.

Our Mission

To create a supportive environment where students can develop their coding skills, share knowledge, participate in competitions, and prepare for successful careers in the tech industry.
